'b'\t\t

Italian-speaking students, native speakers\xc2\xa0or students with previous knowledge of Italian\xc2\xa0must consult the School to be placed in a course corresponding to their level of linguistic competence. Native speakers are not eligible to take language courses by special assessment or to receive advanced credit for them.
