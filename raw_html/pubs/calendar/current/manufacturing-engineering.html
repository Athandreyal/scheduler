Fundamentals of discrete-event simulation including: key concepts; simulation world views; the simulation study life cycle. Modelling and programming aspects of discrete-event simulation including: verification and validation; simulation animation; interfacing simulation software with other systems. Applications of discrete-event simulation to the design and analysis of manufacturing systems.'b''b'\t\t\t
